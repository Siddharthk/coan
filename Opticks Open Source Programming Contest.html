Opticks Open Source Programming Contest - Winner Announcement

The Opticks Open Source Programming Contest is now concluded. We are proud to announce the winner is Nathan Jennings. He submitted an early version of his RADAR Processing plug-in. You can find his source code on Bit Bucket.. His submission included three speckle reducing filters and three texture measures for RADAR images. Thanks to everyone that participated in the contest.
